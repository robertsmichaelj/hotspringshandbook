About

The Hot Springs Handbook was created out of a combination of wanting to learn some new technologies, and frustration with the lack of mordern ways to find hot springs in Idaho. Currently, the main site you find for Idaho Hot Springing has a great wealth of knowledge, but could use a major facelift and for the most part hasn't been updated with new information in 6-7 years. This is my attempt to fix that hole.

A 'Submit' your own hot springs widget is in the works and should be ready soon, along with many other features. In the mean time, if you'd like to send pictures my way, or new information, I'd be very happy to put them up for the world to see.

Feel free to also contact me with the form below or at michael@hotspringshandbook.com, I'd love to hear your thoughts, concerns, and ideas for the site.

Thanks for visiting!
